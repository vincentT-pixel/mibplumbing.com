Who We Are

MIB Plumbing LTD is a UK-registered interactive entertainment company founded by a collective of lifelong gamers who believe the next frontier of human experience lives inside a screen.

We grew up modding, speedrunning, and dreaming in polygons. Now we build the worlds we always wanted to inhabit — immersive, persistent, and endlessly surprising. Our studio blends cutting-edge technology with raw creative passion to craft experiences that don't just entertain, but transform.

Based in London, our small but fierce team operates at the intersection of art, code, and obsession. We are not plumbers of pipes — we are plumbers of digital realities, connecting players to worlds that feel as real as the one outside your window.
